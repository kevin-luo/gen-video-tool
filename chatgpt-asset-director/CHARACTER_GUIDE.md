# Character guide

## Choose the actor mode before generating poses

- Rigid Actor: standing, watching, drifting, reacting, travelling as one cutout.
- Mesh Puppet: small reach, point, nod, look, breathe, small step, seated motion.
- Pose Cut: kick, dive, fall, jump, embrace, turn-around, or any large silhouette
  change.

Pose Cut poses are complete people, not limb sheets. Generate the before and
after poses at the same canvas size, identity, scale, light, costume, and camera
view. Also generate the cover asset that fully hides the switch.

Reject extra fingers/limbs, clipped feet, crossed legs that merge into one,
hands hidden behind the torso, inconsistent left/right roles, floating feet,
goal-facing errors, and impossible support points.
